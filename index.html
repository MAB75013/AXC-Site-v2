Aries X Consulting (AXC) is an independent management consulting firm founded by Mehdi Abdellaoui — a senior consultant with over 12 years of experience transforming complex organisations across Europe.

Mehdi has led digital transformation programs, driven measurable cost reduction, and built Net-Zero roadmaps for some of the world's most demanding organisations — including Retail & Luxury, Automotive, and other global industrial groups.

AXC combines rigorous analytical frameworks with hands-on implementation — bridging the gap between strategy and operational reality.
