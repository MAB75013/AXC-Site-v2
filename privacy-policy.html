05 — Your Rights

GDPR Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — EU 2016/679) and applicable national data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of rectification — have inaccurate information corrected.
  • Right to erasure — request the deletion of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please send your request by email to: contact@ariesxconsulting.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority. In France, this is the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés): www.cnil.fr.
